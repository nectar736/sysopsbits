## What We Can Help With

### ✅ We Love to Hear About:
- Tutorial requests and topic suggestions
- Tool recommendations and reviews
- Guest post opportunities
- Partnership inquiries
- Feedback on our content

### ❌ We Can't Help With:
- Individual troubleshooting support
- Free consulting services
- Job applications (check our careers page instead)

## Guest Posts & Collaborations

We're always looking for experienced sysadmins and DevOps engineers to contribute to SysOpsBits. If you're interested in writing a guest post or collaborating on a project, please email us with:

- Your background and expertise
- Topic ideas you'd like to cover
- Links to previous work (if any)

## Business Inquiries

For business partnerships, sponsored content, or advertising opportunities, please include "Business Inquiry" in your email subject line.

## Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 5 PM EST).

Looking forward to hearing from you!
