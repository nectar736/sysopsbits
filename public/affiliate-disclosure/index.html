Affiliate Disclosure

At SysOpsBits, we believe in complete transparency about how we fund our operations and maintain editorial independence.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that track when someone makes a purchase after clicking through from our site. When this happens, we may earn a small commission at no additional cost to you.

Our Affiliate Policy

✅ What We Do

  • Clearly disclose all affiliate relationships
  • Only recommend products we’ve personally tested
  • Maintain editorial independence - affiliate status never influences our reviews
  • Provide honest assessments of both pros and cons
  • Update recommendations based on current performance and pricing

❌ What We Don’t Do

  • Accept paid placements without disclosure
  • Write positive reviews for products we don’t recommend
  • Let affiliate relationships influence our opinions
  • Prioritize commission rates over product quality

How We Choose Products

Our recommendation process is simple:

  1. Identify a need based on real-world sysadmin challenges
  2. Test multiple solutions in our own environments
  3. Evaluate based on performance, cost, and reliability
  4. Write honest reviews highlighting both strengths and weaknesses
  5. Include affiliate links only for products we genuinely recommend

Current Affiliate Partners

We work with affiliate programs from these types of companies:

  • Cloud Hosting: DigitalOcean, Linode, Hetzner, AWS, Google Cloud
  • VPS Providers: Vultr, Scaleway, OVH
  • SaaS Tools: Monitoring, backup, security platforms
  • Software Companies: DevOps tools, system utilities

Commission Rates

Commission rates vary by company and product type, typically ranging from 10-50% of the first year’s subscription or a one-time fee for hosting services.

These commissions help us:

  • Maintain the website (hosting, domain, security)
  • Invest in testing new tools and services
  • Create high-quality content without paywalls
  • Keep recommendations unbiased (we’re not dependent on any single partner)

Your Support

When you use our affiliate links, you’re supporting our ability to continue providing free, high-quality content to the sysadmin community.

Important: You always pay the same price whether you use our affiliate links or go directly to the vendor. In some cases, we may even be able to offer special discounts through our partnerships.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or editorial policies, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

Thank you for supporting SysOpsBits and helping us keep our content free and independent!
